Belaan cream is a popular skincare product from Turkey, mainly used to treat various skin conditions. It's a thin, white cream with a sweet, pleasant aroma.

The active ingredient, glycyrrhizin, soothes and calms irritated skin, reducing inflammation and redness. Common uses include treating acne, rosacea, and eczema. Some people also use it to reduce fine lines and wrinkles, as well as to soothe sunburns.

Belaan cream can be applied topically, usually two to three times a day, and is often recommended by dermatologists due to its gentle and non-irritating properties.
