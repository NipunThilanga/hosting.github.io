Our Story

Ceytram Holdings (Pvt) Ltd, founded in 1995 in Kalutara, Sri Lanka, is a leading provider of traditional herbal remedies. With a legacy spanning 2500 years, the company specializes in Ayurveda, Unani, Siddha, and homeopathy. Their products, ranging from creams to syrups, address various health issues, ensuring purity and efficacy. Ceytram Holdings aims to expand globally, maintaining its reputation for quality and reliability in the herbal medicine market.
